# Source Quality Rubric

- `0.90-1.00`: peer-reviewed, replicated, primary or authoritative data.
- `0.75-0.89`: strong primary source, high-quality expert source, or official documentation.
- `0.55-0.74`: useful secondary source with limitations.
- `0.30-0.54`: weak support, unsourced expert claim, or uncertain provenance.
- `0.00-0.29`: unsupported, contradicted, promotional, or unreliable.
