# Mnemosyne Content Court Policy v0.1

1. Votes prioritize review and ranking; votes do not directly decide truth.
2. Factual claims require sources or an explicit unsourced/personal status.
3. High-stakes domains require human or domain-reviewer oversight before canonical status.
4. Every accepted graph change must keep proposal, verdict, source audit, and release-note history.
5. Sleep cues must be short, low volume, low emotional activation, and measured against matched controls.
6. Learning-object rank can change based on outcome data, but canonical truth changes require evidence review.
